Note on style: these are real wildlife photos, not the painterly illustrated
style used for the companions and hero cards. That's a deliberate tradeoff —
no single free source covered all 18 species in one consistent illustrated
style, and generating all 18 custom took longer than wanted. If you want a
fully consistent look later, these are the easiest cards to swap since
they're the lowest-rarity tier and each one is a single drop-in file at
`client/public/cards/<id>.jpg`.
